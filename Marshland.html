What is Biodiversity?

explains what Marshland Biodivsersity diversity is

Freshwater habitats are incredibly diverse, with more than 400 large-scale ecoregions and thousands of rivers, lakes, and wetlands. Freshwaters are also hotspots for biodiversity. They are home to at least 140,000 specialist freshwater species!

What harms marshland diversity?

Marshland biodiversity is under attack. Habitat loss from development and pollution shrinks living space, while invasive species muscle out natives. Climate change throws a wrench into water flow and temperatures, stressing these ecosystems. Overfishing and hunting further deplete the rich tapestry of life that marshlands support.

How can we preserve marshland biodiversity?

Marshland biodiversity faces a multi-pronged attack. Habitat loss from development and pollution shrinks living space for native species. Invasive species like Phragmites choke out native vegetation, while predators like Burmese pythons disrupt the food web. Climate change throws a wrench into water flow and temperatures, stressing these ecosystems. Overfishing and hunting further deplete the rich tapestry of life that marshlands support.
